# Padharo Sa Website Redesign

## Overview

Padharo Sa is a premium Rajasthani dining experience operating under the Jain's Little India hospitality group in Mauritius. This project aims to redesign the existing WordPress-based website into a modern, immersive, and experiential web platform built with Next.js and a composable architecture. Rather than functioning as a traditional restaurant website that primarily lists menus and contact information, the new website will serve as a digital extension of the dining experience itself, transporting visitors into the rich heritage, royal hospitality, and culinary craftsmanship of Rajasthan. Every interaction should evoke anticipation, authenticity, and luxury while guiding visitors toward making reservations, exploring the brand story, and engaging with the restaurant's experiences. The platform must combine cinematic storytelling, exceptional performance, accessibility, search engine optimization, and maintainability while remaining cost-effective through an open-source technology stack and modern deployment practices.

---

# Goals

### Goal 1

Create a premium digital experience that immediately communicates Padharo Sa's unique identity as an authentic luxury Rajasthani restaurant rather than a conventional Indian restaurant.

### Goal 2

Increase reservation intent by replacing an informational browsing experience with an emotionally engaging storytelling journey that naturally leads visitors toward booking a table.

### Goal 3

Develop a scalable, maintainable, and developer-friendly architecture using Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, GSAP, BetterAuth, Prisma, and other open-source technologies that significantly reduce long-term maintenance costs compared to the current WordPress implementation.

### Goal 4

Build a reusable component system and design language that can later be adapted across other Jain's Little India brands while preserving each brand's individual personality.

### Goal 5

Achieve excellent technical performance, targeting Lighthouse scores above 90 for Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, and SEO across all major landing pages.

### Goal 6

Establish a structured AI-assisted development workflow using specification-driven development, enabling future features to be implemented consistently through reusable context documents and feature specifications.

---

# Core User Flow

1. Visitor discovers the Padharo Sa website through search engines, advertisements, social media, or direct referrals.

2. Visitor lands on a cinematic homepage that immediately communicates the restaurant's atmosphere through immersive visuals, motion, typography, and storytelling.

3. Visitor experiences the brand narrative and learns about the heritage, philosophy, and authenticity behind Padharo Sa.

4. Visitor explores signature dishes, curated dining experiences, and the restaurant's unique ambience through interactive storytelling sections.

5. Visitor views essential information including operating hours, location, contact details, and reservation availability without interrupting the overall narrative flow.

6. Visitor gains confidence through testimonials, photography, awards, social proof, and premium brand presentation.

7. Visitor completes a reservation inquiry or proceeds to the preferred reservation platform.

8. Visitor receives confirmation and leaves the website with a strong perception of quality, authenticity, and premium hospitality.
